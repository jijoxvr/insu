We Cover for

Kupukoo - a self-service, Peer to peer INSURETECH platform that lets customers use their digital identity to form online networks, share risks and buy insurance products of their choice. Social media will act as the distribution channel.

Accidental Damage

Our Accidental Damage plan covers any damage caused to your phone after an accidental drop or fall. All you require is to upload a video testimonial describing the event to file the claim.

Liquid Damage

We cover liquid damage for any sudden or accidental discharge of water or liquid is caused in your phone. You have to upload a video explaining the incident in order to file claim.

Burglary

Our policy covers the loss of your phone caused by burglars by entering/breaking-in your property unlawfully. We just require the evidence/photo of break-in to file your claim.

Theft

We also cover insurance against loss or damage of smartphone caused by the unlawful taking of your smartphone. For filing a claim you just need to upload a copy of Police Report.

Stolen

We cover the insurance against loss by theft of phone from the person or immediate presence of the possessor. You have to provide the proof of body injury or medical report to file claim.

Why should I choose Kupukoo?

Kupukoo is a Smartphone P2P Insurance company that is transforming the business model of Insurance. Kupukoo platform lets customers use their digital identity to form online networks, share risks and buy insurance products of their choice.

Q
A

How P2P Insurance works?

Customers will be a part of a group, and if no claims are made by the customer or any of the members in their group, then each member of the group receives cashback. If claims are made, there will be no cashback for the customer and their connections. Kupukoo customers don’t ever have to pay more than the premium.

Q
A

What if I want to cancel my policy?

You can cancel your policy at any time through our app or website. Further canceling your policy will eliminate any potential giveback associated with your policy. And if you ever want to come back, we’ll create a new policy for you. Know that our prices change from time to time, so your new policy may come with a different rate.
